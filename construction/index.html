Welcome to FilmUp

FilmUp, LLC is a film market catalyst that promotes nonprofit, professional and student partnerships through films and an exclusive online network. FilmUp offers a vast database of employment, internship, and freelance opportunities. We help make the connections you need to take your career or company to the next level. Our team will match talented filmmakers with projects and companies that are aligned with their skill level and passions.
The FilmUp network not only aims to create meaningful connections but it also aims to produce socially impactful films that support and promote non-profit growth. Our mission is to make the world a better place, one partnership at a time.
